Frequently Asked Questions

I am a beginner. Can I learn these practices?

+

Yes, absolutely! Our Classical Hatha Yoga programs are designed to accommodate practitioners of all levels, including complete beginners. We provide detailed guidance and modifications to ensure everyone can practice safely and effectively.

What is the difference between workshops and Everyday sessions?

+

Everyday sessions focus on regular practice and gradual progression in Classical Hatha Yoga. Workshops are intensive, deep-dive sessions that focus on specific aspects of yoga, offering detailed insights and specialized instruction over an extended period.

Is Inner Engineering mandatory to learn Hatha Yoga?

+

While Inner Engineering provides a wonderful foundation, it is not mandatory to begin your Hatha Yoga journey. You can start with our basic programs and progress at your own pace.

Why Classical Hatha Yoga?

+

Classical Hatha Yoga offers a comprehensive approach to well-being, working with the body, mind, and energy in a systematic way. It maintains the authentic essence of yoga while making it accessible to modern practitioners.
